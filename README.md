GRC SOC 2 → ISO 27001 Starter Kit
Overview

This repository demonstrates a practical, lightweight Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) program designed for a growing SaaS or legal technology company. It is intended to show how an organization can identify and manage risk, align controls across multiple frameworks, support audit readiness, and manage vendor risk as the company scales.

The artifacts in this repository reflect real-world GRC activities commonly required for SOC 2 Type II and ISO/IEC 27001 programs.

What This Project Demonstrates

Enterprise and IT risk identification and tracking

Risk register development and maintenance

SOC 2 and ISO 27001 control alignment and cross-mapping

Policy and procedure governance

Vendor and third-party risk management

Audit readiness and evidence tracking

Executive-level GRC metrics and reporting

Frameworks Covered

SOC 2 (Trust Services Criteria)

ISO/IEC 27001:2022

NIST Cybersecurity Framework (conceptual alignment)

GDPR / CCPA (data protection considerations)

Repository Structure

Risk Management: risk methodology, risk register, and threat modeling

Controls: control catalog, SOC 2 → ISO 27001 crosswalk, control testing

Policies: core security and governance policies

Vendor Risk: vendor tiering, intake questionnaires, and sample assessments

Audit Readiness: SOC 2 and ISO 27001 readiness checklists

Metrics & Reporting: GRC dashboards and reporting templates

Intended Audience

GRC Analysts

Security & Compliance Teams

Risk Managers

Auditors and Consultants

Hiring Managers evaluating GRC experience
